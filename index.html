About

BGMI CHAMPIONSHIP 2025 is a BATTLEGROUND MOBILE INDIA tournament exclusively for college students featuring a total prize pool of over 10k INR. Teams will qualify based on the match results. Play to win and play for fun! A competition of both skills and popularity! Compete over two consecutive weekends to become the official streamer for the region! Winning players from all regions will come together to compete for the role of official GAMER! It is the biggest e-sports event in India and will bring in the biggest sponsors a gaming event has ever seen. So what are you waiting for? It's a squad tournament, just go and register!
